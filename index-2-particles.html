Who am I?

That is a tough question to answer though. I have been a lot of things throughout my life so far. I am a lifelong tech nerd who found his passion for computers early in life playing old school Everquest (kudos to those who know what that is). After that I was a combat medic in the 82nd Airborne, then the GM of a sucessful restaurant startup, and now I have found a way to plug into (pun intended) my original love of technology by becoming a front-end developer. With this less than traditional background, I am able to bring a multitude of talents and experience to any project large or small.

While not at the computer, I can generally be found either at the gym (lifting or BJJ), playing guitar, or hiking with my fiancé and our two dogs.
